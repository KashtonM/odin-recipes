Porcupine Meatballs

Image of completed Porcupine Meatballs

Porcupine Meatballs were a staple food of mine growing up. They were common during the depression. They are easy and quick to make, while still tasting delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup uncooked long grain rice
  • 1 slightly beaten egg
  • 1 tablespoon snipped parsley
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 10 3/4 ounce can condensed tomato soup
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Combine meat, rice, egg, parsley, onion, salt, pepper, and 1/4 cup condensed tomato soup. Mix thoroughly.
  2. Shape in about 20 small meatballs and place in skillet
  3. Mix reamining soup, water, and Worcestershire sauce; pour over meatballs
  4. Bring to boil; reduce heat; cover and simmer for 35 to 40 minutes, stirring often.
